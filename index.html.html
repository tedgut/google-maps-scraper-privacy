Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 31, 2026

1. Overview

Google Maps Batch Scraper ("we", "our", or "the Extension") is a browser extension that helps users collect publicly available business information from Google Maps. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Business Data: The Extension collects publicly available business information from Google Maps, including business names, addresses, phone numbers, websites, ratings, and reviews. This data is publicly accessible on Google Maps and is collected solely for your personal use.
  • Activation Status: We store a license activation status and used serial numbers locally in your browser (using Chrome Storage API) to manage the trial and full version features.
  • User Configuration: We store your search preferences (cities, keywords, and settings) locally in your browser for convenience.

3. Information We Do NOT Collect

We do NOT collect:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
  • Browsing history outside of Google Maps
  • Google account credentials or login information
  • Any data transmitted to external servers — all data processing happens locally in your browser

4. Data Storage

All collected business data and user preferences are stored locally in your browser using the Chrome Storage API. No data is sent to any external server. Data is stored in two locations:

  • chrome.storage.local: Local browser storage (survives browser restarts)
  • chrome.storage.sync: Synced storage tied to your Google account (if signed in)

If you uninstall the Extension, all locally stored data will be permanently deleted. Synced data may persist if you are signed into a Google account.

5. Permissions

The Extension requires the following Chrome permissions:

  • activeTab: To access the current Google Maps tab for data collection
  • scripting: To inject content scripts into Google Maps pages
  • storage: To save collected data and user preferences locally
  • alarms: To manage background tasks and timing
  • tabs: To navigate between tabs during batch collection
  • host_permissions (google.com/maps): To interact with Google Maps pages

6. Third-Party Services

This Extension does NOT integrate with or send data to any third-party services, analytics platforms, or advertising networks. All functionality operates entirely within your browser.

7. Data Export

You can export collected business data as CSV files at any time. Exported files are saved to your local device and are not transmitted anywhere.

8. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data. Since all data is stored locally in your browser, the security of your data depends on the security of your device and browser. We recommend keeping your browser and operating system up to date.

9. Children's Privacy

This Extension is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
